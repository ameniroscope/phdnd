Pip

Race · Class · Level

A small scholar with big ambitions and even bigger sleeves. Pip's grimoire smokes when opened, which is either a feature of the magic or a warning from it — the literature is divided.

Since the strange field beyond Mistbarrow, Pip's bond with root and spore has deepened: the land is no longer only something beneath his feet, but a voice waiting to be asked the right question.
