Pip

Pipcap Sporebliss · Raccoonling · Mushroom Druid, Circle of Spores

Race — Raccoonlings are small forest folk with nimble hands, striped tails, and a knack for scavenging shiny things and rare herbs. They're nocturnal, clever, and often underestimated.

Origin — abandoned as a pup in the heart of the Whispering Forest, Pipcap was lovingly raised by sentient mushrooms and has lived as a hermit in the forest ever since.

Personality

  • Always amused, giggling, slightly high — and very protective of his snacks.
  • Talks to mushrooms through their mycelium network (they are old friends).
  • Finds joy and beauty in rot and decay.
