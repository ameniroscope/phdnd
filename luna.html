Luna

Race · Class · Level

Keeper of the cauldron and reader of star-bound recipes. Luna's brews have never failed — though the definition of "success" is negotiated on a case-by-case basis with whoever drinks them.

From the Copper Kettle Inn to the living city of Thellerkin, Luna has met every impossible thing the road offered with the same steady answer: words first, magic second, and the cauldron for whatever is left.
